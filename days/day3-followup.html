Follow-Up Strategy & Interview Preparation

Overview

Once you’ve made initial contact, a strong follow-up strategy can set you apart. Prepare for interviews by refining your narratives and practicing responses to common technical and behavioral questions.

Strategies

  • Follow-Up Emails: Send a courteous email 24-48 hours after your initial outreach.
  • Interview Preparation: Review your projects, practice articulating your thought process, and prepare for common questions.
  • Storytelling: Craft a compelling narrative that connects your skills, projects, and career aspirations.

Example Follow-Up Email

Subject: Follow-Up on My Application for [Position]

Hi [Hiring Manager Name],

Thank you for considering my application. I’m excited about the possibility of contributing to [Company] and would love to discuss how my experience in prompt engineering and project work can add value to your team.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
