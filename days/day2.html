Day 2: Build & Deploy a High-Value Mini Project

Welcome to Day 2! Today, you'll transition from theory to hands-on development by building a series of practical mini projects. Each project is designed to help you apply prompt engineering techniques in real-world scenarios while mastering API integration, frontend design, and deployment strategies.

We've prepared four distinct mini-projects to cater to different interests and skill sets. Each project overview is available on its own dedicated page, providing detailed instructions, code snippets, and deployment tips. Choose the project that best aligns with your career goals and start building your portfolio!

Project 1: AI-Powered Job Interview Coach

Build an interactive tool that generates tailored interview responses and coaching tips. Learn how to integrate API calls, design effective prompts, and develop a user-friendly interface.

Deployment Strategy

Once you've built your mini project, the final step is deployment. Hosting your project online provides a live URL to share with potential employers and add to your professional portfolio.

  1. Prepare your code repository and ensure your code is well-documented.
  2. Deploy your application using platforms such as Hugging Face Spaces or Streamlit Sharing.
  3. Thoroughly test the deployed app to verify its functionality.
  4. Share your live project link to showcase your skills and creativity.
