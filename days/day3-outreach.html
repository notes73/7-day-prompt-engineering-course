Direct Outreach & Networking

Overview

Proactively reach out to hiring managers and recruiters by crafting personalized messages. Emphasize your unique project experience and technical prowess to capture their attention.

Strategies

  • Personalization: Tailor your emails or LinkedIn messages for each recipient.
  • Storytelling: Highlight specific projects and achievements that align with the company’s needs.
  • Conciseness: Keep your messages brief and impactful.

Example Email Template

Subject: Exploring Opportunities in Prompt Engineering at [Company]

Hi [Hiring Manager Name],

I recently completed an intensive 7-day prompt engineering course where I built an AI-Powered Job Interview Coach. I’m passionate about leveraging language models to drive innovation and would love to discuss how my expertise could benefit [Company].

Best regards,
[Your Name]
