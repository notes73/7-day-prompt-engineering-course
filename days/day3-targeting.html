Targeting High-Impact Opportunities

Overview

Instead of applying en masse, focus on companies and roles where your prompt engineering skills are most valued. Identify organizations that are driving innovation in AI and prioritize opportunities where rapid hiring cycles are common.

Strategies

  • Research: Use LinkedIn, AngelList, and company career pages to locate openings that match your expertise.
  • Referrals: Reach out to current employees and ask for introductions or advice.
  • Prioritization: Target startups and AI-driven companies that value innovation and can make hiring decisions quickly.
